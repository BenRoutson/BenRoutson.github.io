Brideside


About


Brideside is an innovative e-commerce platform that brings you a wide curated collection of bridal party dresses and accessories. This E-commerce website allows brides, bridesmaids and the groom-side to browse and purchase their wedding dresses, gifts, and accessories. Users can search through the pool of varieties, save products for viewing in future, track orders and interact with the assigned stylists.


What/How did?


Designed the web architecture to keep 'Content-to-Commerce' at the heart of its business model and let they fully operate on the motto of delivering best client experience. Focused on the shifting towards delivering an ever-enhanced user-experience, constantly improving the aesthetics of their shopping platform, making it feature-loaded and more and more personalized for every visiting shopper, keeping convenience-to-purchase at the center of everything.

Technology Stack

  • Language: PHP, SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • Framework: Shopify, jQuery
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Ubuntu
